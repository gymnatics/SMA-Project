Data Collection (Excel) Here is our collection of ride data from Uncle Ringo and Disney World, Prof. Nuno :)!

To help us define our ride parameters like ride duration and capacity, we collected ride data on 72 attractions in Disney World and split our simulation’s attractions into three categories:

  1. Slow trains,
  2. Performances, and
  3. Spinning-type rides.

For the three types of rides, we found the mean of each type’s ride duration and capacity before reducing the means to ratios of each other which we will use in the simulation. The performance-type attractions have the longest mean ride duration followed by the slow trains and then the spinning-type attractions. Similarly, the performance-type attractions have the biggest mean capacities, followed by the slow trains and then the spinning-type rides.

See "Data Collection (Excel)" for the full set of data.
